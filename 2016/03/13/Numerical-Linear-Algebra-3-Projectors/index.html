Numerical Linear Algebra 笔记(3) -- Projectors

发表于 2016-03-13   |   分类于 数学   |     |   阅读次数

Projectors

笔记PDF版可以在这里下载。

1. Projectors

A projector is a square matrix $P$ that satisfies

$$
P^2 = P.
$$

2. Complementary Projectors

If $P$ is a projector, $I - P$ is also a projector,
$$
(I-P)^2 = I - P.
$$

The matrix $I - P$ is called the complementary projector to $P$.

Properties:

$$
\text{range}(I - P) = \text{null}(P).
$$

$$
\text{null}(I - P) = \text{range}(P).
$$

$$
\text{range}(P) \cap \text{null}(P) = {0}.
$$

3. Orthogonal Projectors

Theorem 1. A projector $P$ is orthogonal if and only if $P = P^T$.

4. Projection with an Orthogonal Basis

$$
P = QQ^T
$$

where the columns of $Q$ are orthonormal.

5. Projection with an Arbitrary Basis

$A \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}$ whose $j$th column is $a_j$. ${a_1,\ldots,a_n}$ are linearly indendent vectors.

$$
P = A(A^TA)^{-1}A^T.
$$

Reference

  1. Trefethen, Lloyd N., and David Bau III. Numerical linear algebra. Siam, 1997.
